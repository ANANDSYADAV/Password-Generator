User Guide

As we all know that generating a strong paasword is very important to secure our profile in today's world. Here we have given Two options of password generation-
1) Random Generator - It will generate a random password of appropriate length given by the user. Clicking on the Random Generator Button will lead to the appearance of a Prompt Box asking for the length of password needed.
2) Customised Generator - It will generate a custom password for the user. Clicking on the Customised Generator Button will lead to the appearance of a number of Prompt Boxes asking for several specification needed in the password.
