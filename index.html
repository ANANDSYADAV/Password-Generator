User Guide

As we all know, generating a strong password is very important to secure our profile in today's world. Here, have given two options for password generation:

1) Random Generator - It will generate a random password of the appropriate length given by the user. Clicking on the Random Generator button will lead to the appearance of a prompt asking for the length of the password needed.

2) Customised Generator - It will generate a custom password for the user. Clicking on the Customised Generator button will lead to the appearance of a number of prompts asking for several specifications needed in the password.
